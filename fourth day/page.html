Soft Drinks

soft drinks are non-alcoholic beverages that are typically carbonated and sweetened. They come in a variety of flavors, such as cola, lemon-lime, orange, and root beer.

Burgers

A burger, also known as a hamburger, is a popular food item that typically consists of a cooked patty of ground meat (usually beef) placed inside a sliced bun. It is often garnished with various toppings such as lettuce, tomato, cheese, onions, pickles, and condiments like ketchup and mustard.

Pizzas

Pizza is a popular Italian dish that consists of a round, flat base of dough topped with various ingredients such as tomato sauce, cheese, vegetables, and meats. It is typically baked in an oven until the crust is crispy and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Pizza can be customized with a wide range of toppings to suit different tastes and preferences.
